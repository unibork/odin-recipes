Shrimp Scampi

Description

Shrimp Scampi is a delicious and easy way to bring the delights of the sea into the home kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb frozen shrimp
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 white wine
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp parsley, minced

Steps

  1. Thaw the shrimp in cold water and remove the shells.
  2. Drizzle the shrimp with 2 tbsp of olive oil, half of the garlic, 1/2 tsp of kosher salt, and the red pepper flakes. Allow the shrimp to sit for 20 minutes.
  3. Warm 2 tbsp of the extra virgin olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Then add the shrimp to the oven.
  4. Cook for 1 minute, then flip the shrimp and repeat until they become golden brown. Remove the shrimp from the pan.
  5. Melt 3 tbsp of butter in the pan over medium heat and add the remaining garlic. Cook for 30 seconds, then add the white wine and lemon juice. Cook for another five minutes.
  6. Add the shrimp back into the pan and stir in the last tbsp of butter and 1/2 tbsp of kosher salt. Add the minced parsley and cook for 1 minute.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
