Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese, duh. Useful as the best excuse to get out of an awkward conversation.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 slices sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter, softened
  • 1 tbsp parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp basil, chopped
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tsp fresh dill, chopped

Steps

  1. Spread butter over one side of each piece of bread and lay cheese slices on unbuttered side of one piece of bread.
  2. Sprinkle the parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, and dill on the other slide of bread.
  3. Heat a skill using medium heat. When skillet reaches temperature, place sandwich in skillet and cook each side for about 3 minutes or until the cheese melts.
  4. Enjoy!
