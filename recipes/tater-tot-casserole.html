Tater Tot Casserole

Description

Tater tot casserole was a childhood favorite of mine. What's not to like about it? It combines two tasty foods - casserole and tater tots - into one even tastier final product. Plus, it's super easy to prepare and bake.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 lb. frozen tater tots
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 celery, diced
  • 1 can of peas
  • 1 can of mushroom soup
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350F.
  2. Lightly grease and 8x8 baking dish with oil or cooking spray.
  3. Brown the ground beef in a skillet and dispose of excess fat.
  4. Once beef is thoroughly cooked, add onions to skillet and saute until translucent.
  5. Transfer ground beef to baking dish and spread even throughout entire dish.
  6. Transfer mushroom soup on top of ground beef layer.
  7. Layer the peas and celery on top.
  8. Add the tater tots on top in rows as the final topping.
  9. Bake for 1 hour at 350F.
  10. Add salt and pepper as needed and serve immediately.
